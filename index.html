Her Story

Born in Tuscany.
Summoned by
the Cosmos.

Raised in the terracotta-dusted hills of Tuscany, Juliette Angelini heard frequencies others couldn't. The ancient Italian earth hummed beneath her childhood; Renaissance cathedrals reverberated through her bones. Then she found Psytrance—and everything clicked into place.

A decade of underground dedication later, she now stands as one of electronic music's most magnetic forces: raw, spiritual, technically ferocious, and unapologetically feminine. No genre plays games with consciousness quite like hers.

Her signing to Sound of God Recordings—the visionary label of Psytechno legend YEHOSUA HIMSELF—was the industry's worst-kept secret. Now fully unleashed, Juliette is redefining what a psytrance DJ can be, and can mean.
